  • About:

    Triple-L Ranch is a unique and comprehensive phonemic awareness program that teaches you to HEAR and PRODUCE phonemic contrasts which most ELL individuals do not perceive. Triple-L consists of 554 colored drawings, 27 phonemic contrasts, a manual, a screening test, and an aid for non-native English teachers.
